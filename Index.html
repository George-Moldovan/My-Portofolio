A few things about me!

Welcome to my corner of the internet!

I'm delighted to share a bit about myself with you. For the past decade, I've been immersed in the world of photography, specializing in wedding , event and couples photography. Based in the vibrant city of Cluj-Napoca, Romania, I am happy to call this region my home.

Each wedding that I capture brings me immense joy and satisfaction, knowing that I have the privilege of preserving the most precious moments of people's lives.

Beyond photography, I lead a fulfilling life as a devoted husband and proud father of three.

Thank you for visiting my website and taking the time to learn a bit about me and my work. If you'd like to get to know me better or have any questions about my photography services, please don't hesitate to reach out. Feel free to send me an email. Please include details about your event such as the date and location. I look forward to hearing from you and possibly collaborating to capture your special moments!
